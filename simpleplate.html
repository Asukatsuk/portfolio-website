Simple Plate

About

Simple Plate is a web app indroducing time efficient cooking for busy people by offering a variety of fridge/freezer stock-able recipies.

Challenge

We all know cooking at home has a lot of benefits. It's healthier, saves money, bring joy and more. However, busy professionals including myself don't have much time for cooking. We end up picking up or ordering food often. One research shows every time people order food digitally, you subconsciously order more. It's not good for your health and finance in the long run. I would like to create a recipe app will motivate people cooking and have healthy eating habits.

Competitor Analysis

User Research

Objective

I conducted a survey of 4 people. The purpose was to determine the basic ‘pain-points’ of cooking. Working with real world data is a good staring point to help avoid guesswork and provided a better chance to discover the root of the problem and how to solve it.

Method

I asked following 7 questions.

Q1: How many times a week do you cook?
Q2: How often do you go to the grocery shopping?
Q3: How often do you use online recipes or recipe app?
Q4: Is there any specific app or website you use for recipes? How do you like about the app or website?
Q5: What are the key elements of searching a recipe on app or online?
Q6: How important for you to cook at home?
Q7: What’s your experience with using recipe app or website? Have you encountered any problems? If you can improve some features, please describe.

Result Synthesis

Pattern

  • All of candidates think cooking at home is essential for them, and they all have searched for recipes using smartphone or tablet
  • Because of COVID, they all minimize grocery shopping trips
  • They all care about what they eat and healthy eating
  • They all think visual/video will be helpful to learn recipes
  • Easy ingredients or shopping list seem to be another important key to motivate them to cook

Quotes

“The Simpler the better!”
“Kitchen and food shopping aren’t my happy place so anything that could help to save time is a priority!”

Goals/Needs

  • Quick easy video instruction
  • More visuals, fewer words and a quick summary
  • Easy shopping list. ingredients from multiple recipes could get compiled into one big shopping list saves over buying
  • Introducing fridge/freezer stock-able recipes to cook a couple recipes at once and reduce time for cooking
  • Featuring a storage period list to help reducing food waste

Usability Testing

Objective

I conducted an early prototype testing. The goal of this test was to assess how fluid a user’s experience was on mobile app. I wanted to observe if the users could complete 3 basic tasks to measure the application’s basic functionality.

  • Find out if the user can successfully search a recipe
  • Find out if the user can easily use a step-by-step video recipe page
  • Find out if the user can easily find a shopping list
