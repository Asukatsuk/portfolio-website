About

My Story

Having grown up in Tokyo yet having spent last 20 years in LA and NYC gives me an edge in working as a designer.

I’ve experienced working in the fashion industries where I specialized in creating graphics and print patterns from concept to manufacturing. In my role, I researched trends, created moodboards for product development, prepared artworks for sampling, and worked closely with sales team to deliver the best products. After some time in this field, I wanted to focus on designing the products that make a useful effect on people’s lives, and started having strong curiosity to digital products which led me to UI design. Incorporating both my product development experience and creativity, I would like to design simple, attractive and enjoyable products.
